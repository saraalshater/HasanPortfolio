My Journey
with Sound Begins

My journey with sound began in high school when I was creating simple beats on the computer, and that’s when I discovered my passion for audio production

During my university studies, I trained at Hayat FM and gradually advanced to become a broadcast engineer. However, my ambition led me to study sound engineering at SAE Australian College to become an executive producer.

I decided to open 'Tafaseel' (Details) studio because I believe that sound design is like logo design; every detail matters. I love analyzing the things around me and exploring the principles that drive them.

I founded Adam Training Center, which trains students in sound engineering
